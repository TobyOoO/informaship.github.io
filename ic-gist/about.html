National Sun Yat-sen University, Kaohsiung, TAIWAN
October 27th-28th, 2017

Gender in Science and Technology (S&T) is a critical interdisciplinary research domain and has become a much-addressed perspective in scientific policy and research in many countries. To develop this field in Taiwan, the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST) has entrusted the Taiwan Gender in Science & Technology project (Taiwan GIST) to the Graduate Institute of Gender Education of National Kaohsiung Normal University. Based on global Gender Mainstreaming trends, Taiwan GIST attempts to assist government to include “gender balance” and “gendered innovations” thinking into policy and research development. This year, we organize the 2017 International Conference of Gender in Science & Technology and we invite all those interested and involved in gender, science and technology policy or research, to exchange views and opinions and establish new networks with peers and other stakeholders in the field of Gender in S&T. Taiwan GIST is pleased to announce the Call for Proposals for the 2017 International Conference of Gender in Science & Technology, to be held in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, October 27th -28th, 2017.
